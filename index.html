Predicting NFIP Flood Claims

FEMA National Flood Insurance Program · ~500K claims

Built and benchmarked Lasso, Random Forest, and XGBoost models on ~500K cleaned FEMA claims (54 features) to predict flood-insurance payouts — XGBoost reached R² = 0.956, an 80% RMSE reduction over baseline. Used SHAP and Partial Dependence Plots to translate the model into interpretable risk drivers for insurance pricing.
